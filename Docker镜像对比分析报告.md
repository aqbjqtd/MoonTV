## 🎭 生产部署建议

### 推荐配置

```yaml

version: '3.8'
services:
  moontv:
    image: moontv:test
    container_name: moontv-prod
    restart: unless-stopped
    ports:
      - '3000:3000'
    environment:
      - NODE_ENV=production
      - DOCKER_ENV=true
      - PASSWORD=${PASSWORD}
      - NEXT_TELEMETRY_DISABLED=1
    healthcheck:
      test: ['CMD', 'curl', '-f', 'http://localhost:3000/login']
      interval: 30s
      timeout: 10s
      retries: 3
      start_period: 15s
    deploy:
      resources:
        limits:
          memory: 512M
        reservations:
          memory: 256M
```
